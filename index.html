Documentation

Getting Started

Learn how to set up your first stream in minutes.

Step 1: Upload a Video

Go to the Upload section and drag & drop your video (MP4, MOV, or WEBM, max 2GB).

Step 2: Configure Stream

In Live Control, enter your stream keys for YouTube, Facebook, or TikTok. You can enable/disable each platform.

Step 3: Start Streaming

Click Start Stream. The video will be broadcast to the selected platforms. Monitor status in real time.

Uploading Videos

Supported formats, size limits, and metadata editing.

Supported Formats

MP4, MOV, WEBM. Maximum file size: 2GB.

Metadata

After upload, you can edit title, tags, and description in the upload preview panel.

Auto‑deletion

Videos are automatically deleted after 24 hours to maintain privacy and storage.

Live Control

YouTube, Facebook, TikTok configuration.

YouTube

Enter your primary RTMP URL (e.g., rtmp://a.rtmp.youtube.com/live2) and stream key. Backup server is optional.

Facebook

RTMP URL: rtmp://live-api-s.facebook.com:80/rtmp/ and your stream key (starts with FB-).

TikTok

RTMP URL: rtmp://live.tiktok.com/live/ and your stream key from TikTok Live Studio.

Scheduling & Looping

Set timed streams and repeat modes.

Scheduling

Choose a date and time for the stream to start automatically. The system will begin streaming at that moment.

Looping

Select how many times to repeat the video: 1x, 2x, 3x, 4x, Continuous (infinite), 1 hour, or 24 hours.

Auto‑reconnect

If the stream drops, it will automatically try to reconnect.

Overlays & Effects

Watermarks, lower thirds, background music.

Lower Third

Displays a text overlay (e.g., your name or current topic) – coming soon.

Watermark

Adds a logo/image to the corner of your stream.

Countdown

Shows a countdown timer before the stream starts.

Background Music

Provide a URL to an audio file to play in the background.
